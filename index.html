What I do

test method design

Product quality not only has a major impact on customer satisfaction and company reputation. It will also cause financial damage if not thoroughly investigated throughout the production process. Advanced test methods will guarantee the conformity of product specifications while keeping the throughput high. For me as a test developer the challange is to gain a deep understanding of the product and its functionalities without being an expert in every topic. My broad knowledge in many technical fields helps me with that, as well as always keeping close contact with the development department.

test method automation

Once the design and scope have been determined, the goal is to automate as much as possible. Not only does the test duration play a role, but also the elimination of human error. My main competence lies in hardware automation. I started out as a physics engineer writing labiew code to control a huge VUV ellipsometer at the Metrology light source in Berlin. Not only did the software control multiple stepper motors, pumps etc. but also interacted with the undulator and monochromator of the MLS. I continued with LabVIEW code for testing high frequency trandsucer in optical data transmission, but also continued studying other programming languages mostly out of own interest. After obtaining profound knoledge in most of the common languages I recently started to deep dive into PLC sprogramming with the help of structured test, which again is a field oon its own.
