As with engineering I didnt't quite find my love for programming right away. In fact I kind I wasn't a big fan of the programming courses in my engeneering classes. Ever since I suck in everything in can find about programming. Having basic knowledge in basically every program language there is I find myself most interested in embedded programming which combines my interst interest in electrical engeneeringand programming.
